Diving Watches

Rolex Submariner

An iconic diving watch known for its robust build, including a unidirectional rotatable bezel, luminous markers, and high water resistance, making it a staple for professional divers and enthusiasts alike.

Omega Seamaster

The Seamaster series is renowned for its durability and water resistance, featuring a helium escape valve and a rugged design that caters to both recreational and professional divers.


Tag Heuer Aquaracer

This watch combines a sporty design with practical features such as a rotating bezel, luminous hands, and high water resistance, making it ideal for diving and other water activities.

Seiko Prospex

Known for its reliability and affordability, the Prospex series offers robust water resistance, a durable case, and practical features suited for both casual and serious diving.
